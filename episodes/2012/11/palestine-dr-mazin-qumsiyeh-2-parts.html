Palestine: Dr. Mazin Qumsiyeh (2 parts)

With humor and charm, Qumsiyeh presents much needed historical context to the Palestinian struggle for liberation.

In part 2, the Palestinian activist and academic discusses issues such as: the separation wall within Palestine, Israeli settlement blocs around Bethlehem and East Jerusalem, the two-state "illusion", the one-state solution, the Boycott, Divestment and Sanctions (BDS) movement, statehood for Palestine at the UN, etc. (recorded Oct. 27, 2012).
