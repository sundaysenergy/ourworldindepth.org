Beehive Collective: The True Cost of Coal (2 parts)

the beehive collective is a group of artists and educators who, through their unique and elaborate works, share narratives about some of the most expansive and important topics of the day. their newest piece is called "The True Cost of Coal" and centers around the history of industrialization in southern Appalachia. through story-telling and information about the main driving force behind this industrialization, mountain-top removal coal extraction, we get a sense of our own history and likely struggles for our future. filmed April, 2011 (Minneapolis).
